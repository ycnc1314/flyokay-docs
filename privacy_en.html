Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

FLYOKAY attaches great importance to user rights and privacy protection. This Privacy Policy is intended to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide you with better services, we will collect and use necessary information, and adopt industry-leading security measures to protect your information security.

1.1 Device Information

To implement the basic functions of the APP, we need to collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Including device model, operating system version, device identifier (IMEI/Android ID/advertising identifier, etc.), MAC address
  • Bluetooth Information: For device connection and communication
  • Usage Data: Including APP function usage records, preference settings, etc.

1.2 Permission Explanation

Based on your explicit authorization, we may obtain the following permissions:

  • Bluetooth Permission: For searching and connecting FLYOKAY devices
  • Storage Permission: For saving user-created animations and image materials
  • Camera Permission: For taking photos for editing functions

2. Information Storage and Protection

2.1 Data Storage

Your personal information will be stored on secure servers. We use industry-leading security technologies and measures to protect your information security:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage (SSL/TLS encryption)
  • Strict access control mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability fixes

2.2 Data Storage Period

We only retain your personal information for the period necessary to achieve the purposes described in this policy, unless required by laws and regulations.

3. Information Sharing

Without your consent, we will not obtain, share, or provide your personal information from or to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Required by laws and regulations
  • Protecting our rights, property, or safety
  • Sharing with trusted service providers (only for providing services)

4. Children's Privacy Protection

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 14. If we discover that we have collected personal information of children, we will immediately delete the relevant content upon discovery.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Withdraw consent authorization (through device settings)
  • Delete locally stored data (by uninstalling the APP or clearing APP data)

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. In case of major changes, we will notify you through pop-ups, announcements, or other prominent methods within the APP.
